=========================================
      TALLY CLONE - FEATURE LIST
=========================================

1. DASHBOARD & ANALYTICS
-----------------------------------------
- Real-time financial overview (Total Assets, Liquid Cash, Receivables).
- Interactive Chart.js visualizers for Top Inventory Items and Income vs Expense.
- Live feed of recent accounting transactions.

2. COMPANY MANAGEMENT
-----------------------------------------
- Multi-company registration system.
- Dynamic State & City cascading dropdowns powered by the CountriesNow API.
- Strict Regex-based Indian GSTIN validation (auto-uppercase).
- JSON-based structured address storage.

3. LEDGER MANAGEMENT (CHART OF ACCOUNTS)
-----------------------------------------
- Create standard ledgers (Sundry Debtors, Creditors, Bank, Cash, etc.).
- Set opening balances.
- Attach specific customer email addresses directly to Debtors for automated billing.

4. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
-----------------------------------------
- Track stock items, quantities, and pricing.
- Auto-syncs with the Voucher system (stock automatically deducts on Sales and increases on Purchases).
- Estimated stock value dynamically calculated on the Reports page.

5. DOUBLE-ENTRY VOUCHER SYSTEM
-----------------------------------------
- True double-entry bookkeeping (Select Debit and Credit ledgers).
- Supports 6 voucher types: Payment, Receipt, Sales, Purchase, Journal, Contra.
- Live inventory item mapping during Sales/Purchase entries.
- Full CRUD operations with automatic balance reversal upon deletion.

6. AI RECEIPT SCANNER (OCR)
-----------------------------------------
- Upload photos of physical bills/receipts.
- Connects to the Cloud OCR API (ocr.space) to extract text.
- Smart Regex logic automatically identifies and fills in the:
  > Transaction Date
  > Total Amount
  > Vendor Name / Narration
  > Auto-switches the voucher type to "Purchase".

7. BILLING & AUTOMATED PDF INVOICES
-----------------------------------------
- Professional Tax Invoice PDF generation (using fpdf2).
- Automatically includes Company Details, Customer Details, Itemized billing, and calculated Total amounts.

8. LIVE SMTP EMAIL SYSTEM
-----------------------------------------
- Send PDFs and Reports directly from the application via Gmail SMTP.
- Beautiful Email Modals:
  > In the Billing tab: Pulls the customer's saved email from their Ledger (or defaults to your preferred email) and sends the PDF invoice.
  > In the Reports tab: Exports the Dashboard as a PNG and emails the snapshot to any recipient.

9. FINANCIAL REPORTING
-----------------------------------------
- Auto-calculating Balance Sheet.
- Auto-calculating Profit & Loss Statement (Income vs Expenses).
- Real-time Trial Balance with matching Totals.
- Visual Group Distribution pie charts.

10. EXPORT CAPABILITIES
-----------------------------------------
- 1-Click Export to Excel (.xlsx): Downloads Ledgers, Vouchers, and Inventory into distinct spreadsheet tabs.
- 1-Click Export to PNG: Uses html2canvas to take a high-resolution screenshot of the financial reports.

11. PREMIUM UI / UX
-----------------------------------------
- Modern glassmorphism design language (blur backdrops, sleek inputs).
- Elegant, edge-to-edge modals with orange/gold gradient accents.
- Soft micro-animations on buttons and cards.
- Mobile-responsive sidebar navigation.
