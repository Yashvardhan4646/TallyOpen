=========================================
      TALLYOPEN - FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS
=========================================

1. STANDALONE & PORTABLE ENGINE
-----------------------------------------
- 100% Zero-Dependency: No Python or MySQL required.
- Single-Click Launch: Packaged for one-click Windows execution.
- Build-Proof Persistence: Database (tally.db) is stored outside the app folder for safety.
- Auto-Server: Launches a local background server and opens your browser automatically.

2. COMPLIANT BILLING & GST
-----------------------------------------
- Professional Tax Invoices: Beautiful, high-impact PDF generation.
- Detailed Tax Breakdown: Automatic calculation of CGST and SGST (50/50 split).
- Smart Header: Dynamic business branding from your Settings.
- Signature Ready: Built-in signature area for formal authenticity.

3. ROBUST INVENTORY GUARD
-----------------------------------------
- Zero-Stock Validation: Prevents negative inventory. App blocks sales if stock is missing.
- Auto-Calculation: Unit prices and totals are calculated instantly in the billing UI.
- Stock Trends: Dashboard visualizers show your top-moving products.

4. INTEGRATED EMAIL SYSTEM
-----------------------------------------
- Direct Customer Billing: Email PDF invoices directly from the Make a Bill tab.
- Gmail SMTP Integration: Uses secure Google App Passwords.
- Built-in Tester: Verify your email connection instantly in the Settings tab.
- Report Snapshots: Email high-resolution PNG snapshots of your financial status.

5. DOUBLE-ENTRY ACCOUNTING
-----------------------------------------
- Voucher Types: Sales, Purchase, Payment, Receipt, Journal, Contra.
- Smart Ledgers: Sundry Debtors/Creditors with automated balance tracking.
- Balance Reversals: Deleting a voucher automatically fixes ledger balances.

6. OCR BILL SCANNER
-----------------------------------------
- Photo-to-Data: Upload images of physical bills to extract dates and amounts.
- Auto-Fill: Fills the voucher form automatically based on scanned text.
- Vendor Matching: Detects vendor names to speed up bookkeeping.

7. ADVANCED REPORTING & EXPORT
-----------------------------------------
- Financial Health: Balance Sheet, P&L, and Trial Balance updated in real-time.
- Excel Export: Download all your accounting data as a structured spreadsheet.
- Image Export: Save your dashboard or reports as high-quality PNGs.

8. MODERN PREMIUM UI
-----------------------------------------
- Elegant Design: HSL-tailored color palette with "Accent Purple" and "Deep Navy".
- Micro-Animations: Smooth transitions and hover effects for a premium feel.
- Interactive Tooltips: Click "?" icons across the app for instant help.
- Responsive Sidebar: Sleek navigation designed for desktop efficiency.
